# Steps to convert from Slab 2.0 data and provide as Input to WorldBuilder.

Starting at the surface have two points at i (surface) and i+1 (surface + dy contour)
	a. Calculate radius at point i (Re) and point i+1 (Re-depth)
		r(i), r(i+1)
		dist-angle = d/Re
	b. Convert to cartesian coordinates
		 x(i) = r(i)*sin(dist-angle)
		 y(i) = r(i)*cos(dist-angle)
		 repeat for i+1
	c. Calculate the chord length from point i to point i+1 
		C = sqrt( (x(i+1)- x(i))^2 + (y(i+1)- y(i))^2
	d. Calculate the dip angle between point i and i+1 --> opening angle for arc
		theta = phi(i+1) - phi(i)
	e. Calculate radius of circle connecting both points
		R  = C/(2*sin(theta/2))
	f. Calculate arc length from point i to point i+1
		S = R*theta
5. For each segment output dip(i), dip(i+1), arc-length in world-builder format.
